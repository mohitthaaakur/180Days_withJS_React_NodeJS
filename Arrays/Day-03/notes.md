## Arrays - Day 3

### Topics covered
- splice (mutating: remove/insert at index)
- slice (non-mutating: extract sub-array)
- concat (merging arrays, Symbol.isConcatSpreadable)
- forEach (iteration with item, index, array)
- indexOf / lastIndexOf (strict equality, fails on NaN)
- includes (SameValueZero, detects NaN)
